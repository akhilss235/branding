About

What I Actually Do

I work with business owners who are either stuck, growing fast, or starting fresh — and need their brand to do the heavy lifting before any salesperson walks into the room.

Most businesses have a logo. Very few have a brand that converts. The difference isn't aesthetics — it's the strategic layer underneath: how you're positioned, who you're speaking to, and what you make people feel in the first 3 seconds.

Through Misc Archive and Flumenx, I combine brand strategy with full-stack execution — from identity and messaging to digital campaigns, SEO, and automation. We don't just advise; we build and run.

I don't decorate businesses. I build the strategic foundation that makes everything else — your marketing, your sales, your pricing — work harder.
