Case Study
Welgate

30+ Years
of Legacy.

30+Years in Kerala Market
NewMarket — Lifestyle Retail
NewAudience — New Category
The Challenge
A logo familiar to an entire generation in Kerala. You cannot simply discard 30 years of brand equity.
The Question
Rebrand entirely — or reposition strategically?
The Strategy
Reposition. Not Rebrand.
Rebrand says
"Forget
who we were."
Repositioning says
"You know us.
See who we're becoming."
Protect the legacy. Modernize the perception.
Old Spice Starbucks Welgate
A designer gives you a new logo.
A strategist tells you whether you need one.
Repositioning in Action
Old Spice

From your grandfather's
aftershave — to a cult brand.

Same product. Completely new positioning, voice, and audience perception.
They didn't change what they sold. They changed how the world saw them.
Starbucks

From a coffee shop —
to a lifestyle destination.

They stopped selling coffee. They started selling a feeling —
a place people want to be. That's positioning.
